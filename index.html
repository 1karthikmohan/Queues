About Us

VisionLine Architects is a Bangalore-based architecture and interior design firm founded by Arjun Rao. With 15+ years of experience, the firm specializes in creating sustainable, minimalist, and innovative designs for urban and rural spaces. We believe in blending functionality with aesthetics to deliver timeless spaces.
